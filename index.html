London

London is one of my favorite places to travel to. I've been there twice now and still want to go back. I love the big city and the architecture. The overall aesthetic of the city is super fun to be in.

Good things to know before traveling to London

  • Make sure to get the most up-to-date currency (Plastic).
  • Cars drive on the left-hand side of the road.
  • It's a more expensive place to travel to.
  • The underground (subway) is the best way to travel around London.

Some good places to visit while in London

  • Tower of London
  • Big Ben Clock Tower
  • British Museum
  • The London Eye
  • Buckingham Palace

Costa Rica

I've only been to Costa Rica once, but it was one of my favorite experiences. The hot weather and beach life are unmatched. Everyone was super nice and welcoming.

Good things to know before traveling to Costa Rica

  • Pack for super hot weather.
  • For parking at some locations, you tip people to look after your car, so be prepared to tip.

Some good places to visit in Costa Rica

  • Tamarindo Beach

Costa Rica is mainly just a beach vacation. If you're going to travel to Costa Rica, you'll want to visit the beach, do some surfing, and just enjoy the sun!

Paris

Paris is a very fun place to go! It's a very nice city, similar to London with city life. People call it the city of love, so if you want a place to take that special someone, Paris is the place to go.

Good things to know before traveling to Paris

  • Tipping is not required.
  • It's common for attractions to be closed on Monday or Tuesday.
  • Beware of pickpockets.

Some good places to visit in Paris

  • Eiffel Tower
  • The Louvre
  • Walk Along the Champs-Élysées
  • Arc de Triomphe
  • Île de la Cité
